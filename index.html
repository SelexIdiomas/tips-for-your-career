10 Tips for Your Career

1.Get to know yourself+

Before you start off in pursuit of a career that really lights your fire, take some time to do some serious self-exploration. Make a list of all the things in your life that you have really enjoyed.

2.Brainstorm+

Once you have a picture of the things that light your fire, brainstorm ways you could incorporate them into your life. Be creative.

3.Explore+

Ask, ask, ask! Identify people who are knowledgeable and ask if you can pick their brains.

4.Baby steps+

Look for small steps that will bring your passion into your life and keep you moving forward.

5.Identify your obstacles+

Make a list of what is stopping you. Recognizing fears and doubts is the first step to overcoming them.

6.Create a passion posse+

Surround yourself with people who support and inspire you.

7.Redefine success and failure+

Think about what success really means to you and see failure as a learning opportunity.

8.Make a plan+

Create a plan that gives structure to your ideas and goals.

9.Act today+

Don’t wait for the perfect moment. Take action now.

10.Commit+

Say it out loud. Write it down. Make your goal real.
